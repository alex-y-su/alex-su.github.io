How This Site Made and Site Generators

2019-05-10

The older I become, the less I seek for the most innovative answers. Some solutions should suffice, even if they are far from flawless.

For a website with dynamic material, such as a blog or personal page, generation is not required. Also, it is preferable not to pay wherever possible (there is always a better place to use your money than Amazon or your domestic hosting provider).

HUGO (https://gohugo.io) generates this side statically. I investigated two systems: JEKILL and HUGO, however JEKILL utilizes Ruby and HUGO uses Golang. Because I lack considerable expertise in both, my preferred guideline is to take what you or your team is most experienced with, which didn’t work here. Because Go seemed promising, I chose to devote my efforts to HUGO.
