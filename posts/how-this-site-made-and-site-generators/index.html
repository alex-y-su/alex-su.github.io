How This Site Made and Site Generators

May 10, 2019
1 min read

The older I become, the less I search for maximum fresh solutions. Some solutions should be just good enough, even if far from perfect.

For web site like a blog or personal page Wordpress or another CMS with dynamic content, generation is just not needed. Also, it is good not to pay when you can (there is always a better place to use your money than Amazon or your domestic hosting provider).

This side is statically generated by HUGO (https://gohugo.io). There were 2 systems which I considered: JEKILL and HUGO, but JEKILL uses ruby and HUGO uses Golang. I don’t have significant experience in both so, my favorite rule: take what you or your team are more familiar with, didn’t work here. I decided to invest my time into HUGO since Go looks promising.
