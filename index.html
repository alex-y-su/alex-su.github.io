While it’s a recognized fact that these models might produce different outputs for identical datasets, it’s crucial to differentiate between consistency and determinism. Determinism, in a computational context, means that given the same initial conditions, the algorithm will produce the same results every time. However, inherent randomness during training can introduce variability.
